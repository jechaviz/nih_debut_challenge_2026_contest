# Undergraduate Team Required

Status: `external_gate`

NIH DEBUT is not an open company or solo-builder submission. Final submission
must be owned by a qualifying student team.

## Required team profile

- 3-8 undergraduate students.
- Full-time undergraduate enrollment in a U.S. college or university for at
  least one semester or quarter during academic year 2025-2026.
- Team captain who is a U.S. citizen or permanent resident.
- At least one biomedical engineering or bioengineering major if the institution
  has that department.
- Faculty sponsor able to write the required sponsor letter.

## Suggested roles

- Team captain: final portal owner, certification packet, deadline management.
- Clinical need lead: interviews, background research, workflow mapping.
- Biomedical software lead: Vlang core, validation logic, data pipeline.
- Evaluation lead: synthetic cases, test protocol, results figures.
- Accessibility and documentation lead: Section 508 review, video and PDF.
- Faculty sponsor: scope guard, academic ownership and letter.

## Private information not stored here

- Student names, citizenship/permanent-resident answers and signatures.
- Dates of birth or any under-18 parental consent details.
- Faculty sponsor contact details until the team authorizes inclusion.
- Portal credentials or submission dashboard tokens.

## Handoff message

We have a submission-ready biomedical software package for NIH DEBUT 2026. The
student team will own the project, validate the prototype, record the video,
collect signatures and submit the final PDF through the VentureWell/DEBUT portal.
