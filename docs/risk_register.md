# Risk Register

## R1: Ineligible team

- Severity: high.
- Risk: no valid 3-8 undergraduate team is confirmed before 2026-06-05.
- Mitigation: use outreach pack immediately; keep all private team fields out of
  source control until the team authorizes final packet assembly.

## R2: Medical-device overclaim

- Severity: high.
- Risk: reviewers interpret RenalCue as autonomous diagnosis or treatment.
- Mitigation: describe it as clinician-facing decision support prototype; include
  intended-use limits and FDA CDS note.

## R3: Clinical evidence too thin

- Severity: medium.
- Risk: synthetic-only tests look like a coding demo rather than biomedical
  design evidence.
- Mitigation: student team should add clinician/user input and at least one
  workflow interview receipt before final PDF.

## R4: Accessibility gap

- Severity: medium.
- Risk: demo or PDF misses Section 508 expectations.
- Mitigation: use semantic tables, keyboard controls, readable contrast and PDF
  accessibility checks.

## R5: Submission portal friction

- Severity: medium.
- Risk: online portal fields differ from the prepared form map.
- Mitigation: WAIBA playbooks run draft/preflight only and produce receipts; final
  submit remains authorized captain action.
