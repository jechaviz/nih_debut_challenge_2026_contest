# Ethics and Safety Attestation Draft

RenalCue uses synthetic data only. No protected health information, credentials,
portal tokens or private student identity fields are stored in this repository.

The prototype is intended for educational and clinician-facing decision-support
review. It does not diagnose CKD, replace professional judgment or instruct
patients to start, stop or change treatment.

Clinical logic is inspectable: eGFR, GFR category, UACR category, risk band and
recommendation are shown in the evidence record. Future real-world pilots should
use IRB or institutional review guidance when collecting human-subject data or
clinic workflow data.

AI assistance was used to prepare software, draft documentation and automation
plans. The eligible undergraduate team must review, understand and own all final
materials before submission.
