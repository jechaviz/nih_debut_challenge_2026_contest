# Three-Minute Video Outline

Target duration: 3 minutes max.

## 0:00-0:25 Need

Show the community clinic intake problem: CKD is common, underdiagnosed and
silent, while eGFR/UACR results are often scattered across forms and portals.

## 0:25-0:55 Concept

Introduce RenalCue as a biomedical software workflow that validates inputs,
calculates eGFR, stages albuminuria and routes cases with transparent logic.

## 0:55-1:45 Prototype

Screen-record the Vue demo. Filter cases, inspect eGFR and UACR stages, show
risk mix and explain why urgent cases escalate.

## 1:45-2:25 Evaluation

Show the synthetic test set, 100% expected-label pass rate and generated JSON
receipts. Clarify that no PHI is used.

## 2:25-3:00 Impact

Close with adoption path: clinician/nurse review, low-resource screening
workflow, faculty-supervised validation and next step toward a clinic pilot.
