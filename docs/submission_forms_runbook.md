# Submission Forms Runbook

## Portal

Start from https://venturewell.org/debut/ and follow the official DEBUT 2026
submission links.

## Automation role

Automation prepares draft data, checks local artifacts, opens the official page
and records evidence receipts for the authorized captain review session.

## Required human decisions

- Team roster and signatures.
- U.S. citizen/permanent-resident indication.
- Faculty sponsor letter approval.
- Optional VentureWell prize consideration.
- Final upload and submit confirmation.
