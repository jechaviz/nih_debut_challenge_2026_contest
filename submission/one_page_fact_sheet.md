# RenalCue Fact Sheet

## Problem

CKD is common, silent and underdiagnosed. Community screening workflows may have
creatinine, UACR and blood-pressure results but no fast, consistent route from
raw values to clinician action.

## Solution

RenalCue is a low-cost biomedical software prototype that validates kidney-risk
inputs, calculates eGFR, stages albuminuria, assigns a risk band and generates a
clinician handoff receipt.

## Why now

Low-resource clinics and outreach teams need tools that work without expensive
hardware, cloud accounts or opaque AI. RenalCue is transparent and teachable.

## Prototype evidence

- Vlang clinical core.
- Vue3 reviewer demo.
- Eight synthetic CKD triage cases.
- 100% expected-label pass rate.
- Desktop and mobile screenshots.
- WAIBA preflight receipts.
