# Optional VentureWell Appendix Draft

Use only if the undergraduate team opts into VentureWell consideration and the
current official guidelines still allow the expanded narrative.

## Market And Users

Initial users are community clinics, nurse-led screening teams and student-run
health outreach programs that need a low-cost CKD triage workflow. The economic
value is earlier routing and reduced avoidable referral delay, not replacement
of clinicians.

## Regulatory Path

The MVP is framed as clinician-facing decision support and educational
prototype. If commercialized, the team must define intended use, user, claims,
data inputs and whether FDA device software or CDS policy applies.

## IP And Freedom To Operate

The codebase is original project code. The team should run a pre-submission IP
review for name, workflow claims, equations and any third-party assets included
in the final video or PDF.

## Commercialization

The first viable adoption path is a supervised pilot with a community clinic or
university outreach program, followed by evidence of workflow time saved,
clinician acceptance and referral appropriateness.
