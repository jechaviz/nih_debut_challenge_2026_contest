# Undergraduate Team Outreach Pack

Status: `ready_for_student_partner_adoption`

## Ask

We need a 3-8 person undergraduate team enrolled full-time during the 2025-2026 academic year to own, test, record and submit RenalCue to NIH DEBUT 2026.

## What is ready

- Vlang biomedical software core with CKD-EPI, UACR staging, risk bands and validators.
- Synthetic data pipeline, test cases and generated evidence receipts.
- Vue3 CDN +
		SFC + UnoCSS demo for reviewers.
- Draft narrative, checklist, video outline, risk register and form map.

## What the student team must provide privately

- Team roster, signatures, citizenship/permanent-resident indication and under-18 consent if applicable.
- Faculty sponsor letter on department letterhead.
- Final video URL and any real stakeholder support letters.
- Final approval before online submission.
