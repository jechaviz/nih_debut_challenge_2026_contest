## NIH DEBUT 2026

Source: https://www.nih.gov/challenges/2026-design-biomedical-undergraduate-teams-debut-challenge

- Submission deadline is 2026-06-05 at 11:59 PM EDT.
- Teams require 3-8 undergraduate students.
- Team captain must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident.
- Project must solve an unmet clinical or health care need.
- Required package includes one PDF, signed certification form, project
  narrative, faculty sponsor letter and a 3-minute video link.
